Welcome to my lair !

UI / UX Designer, Web Developer & AI enthusiast

Have you heard of the Ambivalent Monkey? If not, let me introduce myself! I'm not your typical code monkey, first of all I'd like to emphasize my attachment to respect and decency. Besides, I'm a designer and a programmer who enjoys taking on new challenges and staying up-to-date with cutting-edge technology. Have you seen some of the recent advances in AI? These new tools are shaking-up the industry, and I believe that developers using AI will be the ones staying in the race.

Now, I know what you might be thinking - "But Ambivalent Monkey, how can you be an expert in everything if you're always learning something new?" Well, being ambivalent means that I'm not tied down to any one specific way of thinking, and I enjoy approaching problems from different angles. If you need top-notch web design that keeps up with evolving technology, look no further than the Ambivalent Monkey! My creative approach means that I can seamlessly blend inspirational new designs with well established patterns - resulting in visually stunning and functional websites.
